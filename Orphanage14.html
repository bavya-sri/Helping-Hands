Mercy Home for Boys & Girls

Needs : Food, Sanitory Napkins

Mercy Home for Boys & Girls is a Catholic organization answering the Gospel call to minister to children and families in need. Each year, more and more children need care outside of their own homes simply to survive. Many no longer have a place that they can call home. At the same time, funding for not-for-profits that can provide children in need with a safe place to live is getting more and more scarce.
