They fight for every child to feel the love and belonging of a safe family home. Since 1994, Hope and Homes for Children has been working to stop the institutionalization of children. There are 200 people, in multiple countries, inspiring organizations, including the UN, EU, and governments around the world, to close the doors of orphanages forever.
